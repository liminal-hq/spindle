Concept 1 / The Concourse

A grand arrival hall that guides the user from structure, to scene, to remote logic, to a trustworthy build.

This concept treats the editor like a well-signed transit interchange. Every important task has a platform: menu scope on the left, authored scene at centre, and context-aware controls on the right. Advanced tools are visible, but they read as destinations rather than clutter.
