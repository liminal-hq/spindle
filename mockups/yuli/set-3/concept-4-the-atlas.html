Concept 4 / The Atlas

A map-making workspace for multi-menu projects where generation, pagination, titleset boundaries, and authored edits stay legible.

The Atlas is designed for projects that outgrow a single canvas view. It treats each menu as part of a navigable territory: global menus, titleset menus, chapter sets, and setup branches are all shown in relation to one another. The current scene still gets a large working surface, but the surrounding layout explains where it lives in the disc.
