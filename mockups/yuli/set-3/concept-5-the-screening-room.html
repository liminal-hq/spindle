Concept 5 / The Screening Room

A cinematic review space that keeps authored ambition and DVD reality side by side, without making compromise feel punitive.

The Screening Room is built around trust. Instead of hiding the compiler behind a final step, it lets the user compare authored intent, compiled output, motion timing, palette pressure, and route behaviour in the same visual frame. It is a room for refinement rather than surprise.
