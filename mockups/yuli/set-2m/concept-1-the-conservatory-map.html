Merged Concept / The Conservatory Map

A menu editor that treats templates as helpful seeds, menus as a navigable collection, and the canvas as the user’s real home.

The left column becomes a hybrid rail: all authored menus are always available for quick switching, while templates and reusable starters live alongside them as optional seeds. The centre can shift between a rich design studio and a menu-map view because those two perspectives serve different purposes. The right side stays focused on the currently selected menu and selected node.
