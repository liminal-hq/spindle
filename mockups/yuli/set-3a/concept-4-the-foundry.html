Concept 4 / The Foundry

A grounded generator-focused workspace for building chapter grids, setup menus, and titleset-aware menu families without leaving the current app’s visual vocabulary.

The Foundry is the most production-minded concept in this set. It assumes menu generation becomes a first-class workflow in Spindle and gives it a proper home, but still uses the same cards, muted surfaces, and orange-accented cues the app already has today.
