Concept 1 / The Dockyard

A heavier-duty version of today’s scene editor that feels ready for real work without losing Spindle’s calm dark workstation tone.

The Dockyard keeps the familiar left menu list, central canvas, and right inspector, but gives each zone more operational clarity. It makes generated menu families, scope, and compile state easier to scan while staying visually close to the live app.
