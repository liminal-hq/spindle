Concept 5 / The Booth

A compile-review booth that feels native to today’s app and explains how authored menus become real DVD output before the user hits Build.

The Booth is the most practical extension of Spindle’s current Build and Menus surfaces. It puts authored preview, compiled preview, motion timing, and actionable diagnostics in one place, but keeps the same cards, small type, and operational tone the app already uses.
