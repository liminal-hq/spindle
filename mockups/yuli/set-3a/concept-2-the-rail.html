Concept 2 / The Rail

A mode-guided variant that keeps the current app shell intact while making each authoring step feel more deliberate and less crowded.

The Rail assumes the underlying editor stays the same, but the top of the page becomes a guided strip: Design, Bind, Remote, and Compile each show the most relevant controls first. The result is still recognisably Spindle, just calmer in motion.
