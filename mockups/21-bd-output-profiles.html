Video Codec
Blu-ray supports three video codecs. H.264 AVC is the most common for authored content.
H.264 AVC
Best balance of quality and compatibility. Mandatory decoder on all BD players. Recommended for most projects.
Recommended

Higher compression efficiency. Supported on UHD Blu-ray players. Best for 4K or high-bitrate content.
